The really fine print

Privacy.

What this site does (and mostly doesn't do) with your data.

The short version: this site has no cookies, no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no accounts. It's a static page. Fonts and images are served from this site itself — nothing is loaded from third-party CDNs.

Controller

The controller within the meaning of the GDPR is the operator of this site, reachable via GitHub.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, a service of Cloudflare, Inc., 101 Townsend St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA. When you visit the site, Cloudflare technically processes your IP address and standard request metadata (browser type, time of access, requested page) to deliver the site securely and reliably. This processing is based on our legitimate interest in operating a fast, secure website (Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR). Cloudflare participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework. More in Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Downloads & external links

The download buttons and repository links lead to GitHub (GitHub, Inc.). Once you follow them, GitHub's privacy statement applies. This site itself transmits nothing to GitHub while you're just browsing.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection, as well as the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Since this site stores no personal data about you, there is usually nothing to hand over — but you can always reach out by opening an issue on GitHub.
