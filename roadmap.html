The roadmap

Where next?

Mural today is a little gallery that lives on your Mac. The plan: turn it into a wall we all paint together — a community place where anyone can publish their wallpapers.

A native home

Shipped · v1.0

The app as it is today — and the foundation everything else builds on.

  • A hand-picked gallery of stills, plus procedural wallpapers.
  • Mural Studio — drawn backdrops, palettes, scrapbook stickers.
  • Video wallpapers on the Desktop & Lock Screen (macOS 26).

The Mural server

In the works

A small, quiet home on the internet for wallpapers. The app checks in, and fresh walls simply appear in the box — no account needed to browse.

  • A public wallpaper feed the app can pull from.
  • New curated drops without waiting for an app update.

Community wallpapers

The goal

The whole point of the journey: anyone can publish. Sketch something lovely in Mural Studio, hit share, and it hangs on the community wall for everyone to take home.

  • Upload straight from Mural Studio or your own files.
  • Human-reviewed submissions, so the gallery stays hand-picked — never a dumping ground.
  • Credit where it's due: every wall carries its maker's name.

Wherever you take it

Someday

Themed packs, seasonal drops, collab collections — once the community wall exists, the community decides what hangs on it.
